# 4G vs 5G in Kenya — Is 5G Worth It?

5G coverage is growing across Nairobi and major towns, and more phones now ship with it. But should it drive your buying decision today? Here is a practical view.

## What 5G actually gives you

- **Much faster downloads** where coverage is strong — large files and HD streaming load almost instantly.
- **Lower latency**, which helps with video calls and online gaming.
- **Better performance in crowded areas** like stadiums or events, where 4G gets congested.

## The honest reality in Kenya

5G coverage is still concentrated in parts of Nairobi and select urban centres. If you live or work outside those areas, you will spend most of your time on 4G regardless of what your phone supports. And good 4G is already fast enough for streaming, social media, M-Pesa and video calls.

## So should you buy a 5G phone?

- **If you are buying mid-range or above (KSh 40k+):** yes — most phones at that price include 5G anyway, so you future-proof at no real extra cost.
- **If you are on a tight budget (under KSh 20k):** don't pay a premium for 5G. A better battery, more RAM or a nicer screen will improve your daily experience far more.

## The bottom line

Buy 5G if it comes included at your budget, but never stretch your budget *just* for it today. Coverage will keep expanding, and a great 4G phone will serve you well for years. [Compare phones across stores](/c/smartphones) and weigh battery and RAM first.
