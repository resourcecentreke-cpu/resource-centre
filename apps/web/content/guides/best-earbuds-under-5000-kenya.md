# Best Wireless Earbuds Under KSh 5,000 in Kenya

Budget true-wireless earbuds have improved dramatically. Under KSh 5,000 you can get clean sound, decent battery and even noise cancellation — if you know what to look for.

## Features worth caring about

- **Battery life.** Look for 6+ hours in the buds and 24+ hours with the case. Brands like Oraimo and Xiaomi do well here.
- **Bluetooth 5.2 or newer.** Better connection stability and lower lag for video and calls.
- **Call quality.** If you take a lot of calls, look for earbuds with multiple microphones and noise reduction — cheaper buds often sound muffled to the other person.
- **Comfort and fit.** In-ear tips that seal well give you far better bass than loose-fitting buds, at no extra cost.

## Features you can skip at this price

Active noise cancellation (ANC) exists on some budget buds but is usually weak — treat it as a bonus, not a deciding factor. Fancy app equalisers are nice but rarely essential.

## Smart buying

Oraimo, Xiaomi (Redmi Buds) and Realme dominate this band on value. [Compare earbud prices across stores](/c/earbuds) before buying — the same pair can differ by KSh 1,000 between shops, and bundle deals are common.
