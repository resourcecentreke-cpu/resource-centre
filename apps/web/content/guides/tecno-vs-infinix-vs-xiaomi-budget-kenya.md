# Tecno vs Infinix vs Xiaomi — Which Budget Brand Wins?

These three brands dominate Kenya's budget and lower-mid-range market. They overlap a lot, but each has a personality. Here is how they compare.

## Tecno

Tecno builds specifically for African markets, with big batteries, bright screens and strong retail presence countrywide. Software is feature-packed but heavier, with more pre-installed apps. Great for buyers who want a lot of phone and easy local service.

## Infinix

Closely related to Tecno, Infinix often pushes a little harder on charging speed and gaming-friendly screens at the same price. Software is similar. A good pick if you want fast charging and a smooth display on a budget.

## Xiaomi (Redmi)

Xiaomi's Redmi line usually offers the **cleanest software**, the best displays, and strong processors for the money. The trade-off is slightly smaller retail/service presence in some towns and occasional ads in the software. Often the best all-rounder if you value a tidy experience.

## How to choose

- **Want the biggest battery and easiest local service?** Tecno.
- **Want fast charging and a gaming-friendly screen cheaply?** Infinix.
- **Want the cleanest software and best screen-per-shilling?** Xiaomi.

All three are good value — the right pick depends on which trade-off you care about. [Compare current prices across stores](/c/smartphones) before deciding, since promotions shift the value ranking month to month.
