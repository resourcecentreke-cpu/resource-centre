## The honest bottom line

- **✅ galleries** (Samsung, OnePlus, Lenovo, LG, Nothing) cover your highest-value
  hero/flagship/TV products — start there, it's the quickest win.
- **◑ / ▢** brands have no bulk gallery — you'd save images one product page at a
  time, which is slow across ~300 items.
- **The only realistic way to photo-cover all 388** — especially accessories,
  appliances, and budget phones — is your **affiliate product feed**. AliExpress
  (already reachable, application in progress) ships **licensed images + prices**
  in bulk and auto-fills every card. That's the route I recommend for full coverage;
  use the galleries above to hand-pick photos for your flagship/hero products now.
